Hi Bestie! 💖

Hi, I'm Nuzaiba — a third-year, first-semester BSc in CSE student at the University of Asia Pacific. As the Class Representative, I value leadership, teamwork, and fostering a supportive learning environment for my peers.

I'm passionate about Data Science and Machine Learning, always eager to explore innovative ideas and learn something new every day.

With over a year of experience as a creative designer, I specialize in crafting websites that are not just functional, but irresistibly cute and captivating—because I believe every pixel should sparkle! ✨

I actively contribute to campus life as a volunteer at DSW, UAP and serve as the Junior Event Coordinator of the CES Cultural Club, UAP, where I help organize engaging programs that bring our student community together.

Beyond academics and design, I’m driven by a commitment to positive change and community growth through volunteering and creative collaboration.
